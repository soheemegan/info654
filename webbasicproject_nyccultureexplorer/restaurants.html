Exploring the city through restaurants 🍽️✨

This page highlights a few of my favorite restaurants in New York City — spaces that combine design, atmosphere, and taste. Each one reminds me how a meal can be more than food — it’s composition, light, and rhythm all at once.

Restaurants Feature Section

La Mercerie

Address: 53 Howard St, New York, NY 10013

La Mercerie sits inside Roman and Williams Guild — half restaurant, half design studio. Its pale linens, ceramics, and natural light feel curated yet effortless, a reminder that hospitality can be a form of design storytelling.

Boucherie

Address: 99 7th Ave S, New York, NY 10014

Boucherie in the West Village feels like a Parisian daydream — brass details, mirrored walls, and a constant hum of conversation. I love how its space captures that warm intersection between elegance and energy.
