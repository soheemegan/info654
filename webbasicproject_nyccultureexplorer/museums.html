Exploring the city through museums 🏛️✨

This page features a few of my favorite museums in New York — spaces that preserve history while constantly reinventing how we experience it. From medieval cloisters to modern glass façades, each museum offers a different lens on art, architecture, and reflection.

Museums Feature Section

The Met Cloisters

Address: 99 Margaret Corbin Dr, New York, NY 10040

Perched above the Hudson River, The Met Cloisters feels far from Manhattan. I love its serenity — the way medieval architecture and gardens slow you down and invite you to look closer. It’s a reminder that design, even centuries old, still shapes emotion and time.

The Museum of Modern Art (MoMA)

Address: 11 W 53rd St, New York, NY 10019

MoMA never feels the same twice. Between digital installations, iconic modern works, and quiet corners that mix light and structure, it’s one of those places where design theory, emotion, and innovation coexist naturally.
