Exploring the city through cafés ☕✨

This page highlights a few of my favorite cafés in New York City — places that blend atmosphere, design, and rhythm into the everyday. Each spot offers its own story, showing how space and experience shape how we connect, create, and pause.

Cafés Feature Section

Ralph’s Coffee

Address: 888 Madison Ave, New York, NY 10021

Nestled inside Ralph Lauren’s flagship on Madison Ave, Ralph’s Coffee feels timeless. Its rich green interiors and leather details create a quiet luxury that turns a simple coffee into a slow ritual.

12 Matcha

Address: 54 Bond St, New York, NY 10012

Minimal, calm, and intentional — 12 Matcha celebrates design in stillness. Its clean palette and focused menu reflect the quiet precision of Japanese craft, reimagined for downtown New York.
